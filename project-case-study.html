Full-Stack Movie Application Case Study

Role: Full stack developer — Solo lead

  • Timeline: 6 weeks
  • Tools: Canva, Visual Studio Code, Word

Process

Research

Conducted analytics review and 8 user interviews. Found users were confused by shipping costs and multi-step layout which created friction.

Ideation & prototyping

Reduced micro-steps, added inline shipping cost estimator, clarified CTA labels, and ran 5 usability sessions on the prototype.

Solution

Delivered a simplified single-page checkout with progressive disclosure and clearer affordances. Handoff included tokens and specs for engineering.

Impact

  • Checkout abandonment decreased by 18%
  • Average checkout time reduced from 2.5 min to 1.9 min
  • Positive qualitative feedback in follow-up tests

What I learned

Small changes to information architecture and trust signals can have measurable impact; always validate assumptions with quick usability rounds.
